Course Catalogue

Train for a trade, a ministry, or a stronger life.

Seven hands-on programs taught by certified instructors and partner universities — from state-licensed trades to ministry and practical life skills.

01
Education · State License

Teaching

Pedagogical Course · Partner Universities

Complete this program through a partner university — Bob Jones, Arizona State, or Liberty — and earn a state-recognized teaching license valid for any U.S. public, charter, or private school.

  • A real U.S. teaching license recognized nationwide
  • Offered through accredited partner universities
  • Opens doors to stable careers in education
02
Health · Certification

First Medical Aid

In partnership with Med Outreach — South Carolina

A hands-on emergency response course offered with Med Outreach CPR. Learn CPR, first-aid protocols, and how to respond confidently when seconds count.

  • Certification recognized across South Carolina
  • Practical training with certified instructors
  • Skills that can save a life at home, church, or work
03
Trade · 2-Year Program

HVAC Technician

2-Year Program · State License

A two-year hands-on program preparing students to pass the state licensing exam and become certified HVAC technicians — qualified to install and service systems across the U.S.

  • Graduate with a state-issued professional license
  • High-demand trade with strong earning potential
  • Train on real residential & commercial systems
04
Trade · Pre-License

Basic Electrical

Pre-License · 1 Year

A one-year in-person foundation course covering the principles, applications, and electrical loads behind everyday systems — the essential knowledge to pursue a full electrician's license.

  • Solid foundation for a licensed electrician career
  • Covers real-world applications from day one
  • Affordable entry into a high-demand trade
05
Ministry · Performance

Musical Ministry

Voice · Ensemble · Performance

A comprehensive music program covering choral singing, solo vocal performance, voice coaching, and ensemble work — building technique, confidence, and artistry for ministry or stage.

  • Individual voice placement and coaching included
  • Choir, solo, and band ensemble tracks available
  • Grow your musical gift for ministry and community
06
Ministry · Education

Kids Ministry

Sunday School · Teenagers · Youth

Essential training for anyone working with children and youth — Sunday school methodology, teen engagement, and youth program leadership that equips volunteers and staff to make a lasting impact.

  • Practical tools for kids, teens, and youth
  • Equips both volunteers and ministry staff
  • Build programs that keep the next generation engaged
07
Life Skills · Literacy

Finances

Personal & American Financial System

A foundational course on how the American financial system works — banking, credit, stock markets, investing, and real estate basics — to build lasting financial literacy and stability.

  • Understand banks and credit scores, and use them wisely
  • Introduction to stocks, investments, and real estate
  • Practical knowledge applicable from day one
